1. THE IMMEDIATE ("I") PHASE LAYER: SENSORY GRIT EXTRACTION
===============================================================================
CRITICAL GOVERNANCE RULE: Filter out all hindsight. Do not include anything you learned *after* this moment. Describe the scene exactly as it felt when you were standing on the ground inside the chaos, confusion, or reality of the event.

[Parameter 1.1: Temperature & Climate]
Describe the precise physical temperature. Was there a bitter draft coming under a door? Was the humidity sticking to your shirt? Did the air taste like iron or dust?
> 

[Parameter 1.2: Ambient Audio Frequencies]
What were the immediate background sounds? Was there a mechanical hum, a rhythmic ticking, a roaring engine, whispering voices, a sudden silence, or the crunch of dirt beneath your boots?
> 

[Parameter 1.3: Distinct Environmental Scents]
What did the space smell like? Pinpoint the chemical, natural, or environmental odors—grease, spent kerosene, rain on hot asphalt, old paper, wood shavings, or fresh pine.
> 

[Parameter 1.4: Immediate Gut Reactions & Psychological Constraints]
What was your raw, unedited internal reaction *in that exact second*? Capture the confusion, the fear, the adrenaline, the split-second decision, or the sudden quiet focus before you had time to think about the outcome.
